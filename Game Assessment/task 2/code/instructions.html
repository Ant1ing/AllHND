Instructions

Welcome to Body Builder Puzzle Adventure!

In this game, you will assemble various body parts to complete a character. Each level will have a set of body parts that need to be dragged and dropped into the correct positions.

Here are some tips to get started:

  • Drag each body part to its matching outline.
  • Correctly placed parts will snap into place.
  • Complete the character to earn stars and advance to the next level.

Have fun and good luck!
